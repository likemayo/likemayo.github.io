Participating in NYU Tech Venture Workshop

For the ChatSign Technology tech startup: The Tech Venture Workshop is the first phase of the three-part NYU Tech Venture Program. Sponsored by the National Science Foundation, the Tech Venture Workshop (TVW) is a three half-day immersive program where NYU faculty, and their PhD student/postdoc teams learn how to test the commercial potential of their research directly with customers and partners.

Participating in Ma'an Social Incubator

Also for ChatSign Technology: Ma'an's Social Incubator nurtures and invests in social enterprises that demonstrate a clear ability to deliver sustainable social impact by addressing social priorities in education, social, health, and the environment. Twice a year, MSI welcomes 10-12 promising startups, providing them with the essential resources and unwavering support to empower youth and contribute to community development.
