SignTok - 聋哑人专属短视频平台 SignTok - Short Video Platform for Deaf Community

设计工具： Design Tools: Figma

为聋哑人群体定制的短视频社交平台，融合了抖音的交互模式与手语交流的特点。 A short video social platform tailored for the deaf community, combining TikTok's interaction model with sign language communication.

交互原型 Interactive Prototype

完整的 SignTok 应用交互原型。您可以直接在这里体验用户界面的交互流程，包括登录、浏览视频、发布内容等核心功能。 Complete SignTok app interactive prototype. You can experience the user interface flow directly here, including login, video browsing, content publishing, and other core features.

NomNom - 共情数字伴侣 NomNom - Empathetic Digital Companion

设计工具： Design Tools: Figma

NomNom 不仅仅是一个数字助手，更是一个真正理解你的心灵伴侣。它通过共情式交互，帮助用户培养心理健康，实现更平衡、更健康的生活方式。 NomNom is a digital partner that not only listens but truly understands, helping you foster mental well-being and a healthier, more balanced life.

交互功能展示 Interactive Features

Flow 1 & 2: 智能聊天功能 Flow 1 & 2: Smart Chat Features

展示了 NomNom 的核心聊天功能，包括情感识别、共情回应和个性化建议。通过自然的对话方式，帮助用户表达情感并获得心理支持。 Showcases NomNom's core chat functionality, including emotion recognition, empathetic responses, and personalized suggestions. Helps users express feelings and receive psychological support through natural conversation.

Flow 3: 使用流程与界面展示 Flow 3: Usage Workflow & Interface Demo

完整展示了用户使用 NomNom 的工作流程，从初次见面到日常互动，以及各种功能界面的设计细节。 Demonstrates the complete user workflow with NomNom, from first meeting to daily interactions, along with detailed interface designs for various features.
