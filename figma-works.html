ChatSign - 聋哑人无障碍通信平台 ChatSign - Accessible Communication Platform for Deaf Community

设计工具： Design Tools: Figma

ChatSign 是一个综合性的聋哑人通信平台，包含三个核心产品：SignTok（短视频社交平台）、SignCall（视频通话应用）和 SignAvatar（虚拟手语翻译）。该平台旨在为聋哑人群体提供全方位的无障碍通信解决方案。 ChatSign is a comprehensive communication platform for the deaf community, featuring three core products: SignTok (short video social platform), SignCall (video call application), and SignAvatar (virtual sign language translator). This platform aims to provide a complete accessible communication solution.

SignTok - 短视频社交平台 SignTok - Short Video Social Platform

SignTok 是一个专为聋哑人设计的短视频社交平台。它融合了抖音的交互模式与手语交流的特点，让用户可以轻松创作、分享和观看手语视频内容。平台特别优化了视频播放控制和手语表达的细节，确保内容清晰可见。 SignTok is a short video social platform specifically designed for the deaf community. It combines TikTok's interaction model with sign language communication features, allowing users to easily create, share, and watch sign language video content. The platform is specially optimized for video playback control and sign language expression details.

SignCall - 视频通话应用 SignCall - Video Call Application

SignCall 是一款专门为聋哑人设计的视频通话应用。它提供高清视频通话功能，并集成了手语识别和实时翻译功能，帮助聋哑人与听力正常的人进行无障碍沟通。应用特别优化了视频画面的稳定性和清晰度，确保手语动作传达准确。 SignCall is a video call application specifically designed for the deaf community. It provides HD video calling with integrated sign language recognition and real-time translation features, enabling barrier-free communication between deaf and hearing people. The app is specially optimized for video stability and clarity to ensure accurate sign language expression.

SignAvatar - AI 手语翻译虚拟人 SignAvatar - AI Sign Language Translation Avatar

SignAvatar 是一个创新的 AI 手语翻译解决方案，专为提升聋哑人观看视频内容的体验而设计。它在视频播放界面下方实时生成虚拟人物，将音频内容转换为流畅自然的手语动作。这使得聋哑人可以轻松观看电影、电视节目等视频内容，不再受限于传统字幕的表达局限性。虚拟人物的手语表达准确、生动，让观看体验更加自然舒适。 SignAvatar is an innovative AI sign language translation solution designed to enhance the video viewing experience for the deaf community. It generates a virtual avatar at the bottom of the video interface, converting audio content into smooth and natural sign language gestures in real-time. This enables deaf people to easily enjoy movies, TV shows, and other video content without being limited by traditional subtitles. The avatar's sign language expression is accurate and vivid, making the viewing experience more natural and comfortable.

设计工具： Design Tools: Figma
